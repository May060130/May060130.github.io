Lifetime Achievement Major Work

Tom Holland

brief introduction

Thomas Stanley Holland (born 1 June 1996)is an English actor. An alumnus of the BRIT School for Performing Arts and Technology in London, he began his acting career on the West End stage in the title role of Billy Elliot the Musical at the Victoria Palace Theatre from 2008 to 2010.

Holland achieved international recognition when he portrayed Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe superhero films, beginning with Captain America: Civil War (2016), Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017), Avengers: Infinity War (2018), Avengers: Endgame (2019), Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019) and Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021). In 2017, at the age of 20, he won the BAFTA Rising Star Award, becoming the award's second-youngest recipient at the time.

Holland resides in Kingston upon Thames in south west London, near the house of his parents and younger brothers. He has a blue Staffordshire Bull Terrier named Tessa. He was diagnosed with dyslexia at age seven. He is a supporter of Tottenham Hotspur. In 2021, it was revealed that Holland and his Spider-Man co-star Zendaya were in a relationship.
